About

Image Tagger is an application used for marking images. It cycles through a directory of corn kernel images, allowing users to mark the outline of the embryo. This was developed by Jacob Stimes in the Ganapathysubramanian group (www.me.iastate.edu/bglab). If you experience any issues, have questions, or would like to share feedback, please reach out to Jacob at jstimes@iastate.edu or Baskar Ganapathysubramanian at baskarg@iastate.edu.

Funding from the Plant Science Institute and ISU PIIR is acknowledged.

General Instructions:

  1. After logging in with a username, the program is already in marking mode.
  2. Use the mouse cursor to draw a region of interest on the picture currently displayed.
  3. Once you are done marking an image, click 'Save/Next Image'. The marked image will be replaced with the next one, and all data is reset. Continue this procedure to keep marking images, your progress is shown in the upper right corner.
  4. A message will be shown when you have marked all images.

Options and Controls:

  • 'Clear' - resets all markings and data for the current image
  • 'Undo' - removes the last drawn marking, and data associated with it
  • 'Save/Next Image' - Saves markings on current image and moves on to the next image.
